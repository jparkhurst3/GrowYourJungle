Crassula Ovata

Also known as the jade plant or money plant, this is a low-maintenance plant that's great for sunny windows and beginners.

  • Water 1-2 times a week
  • Keep near a bright window

Dracaena Trifasciata

Commonly known as the snake plant, it is a popular houseplant because it is tolerant of low light levels and irregular watering.

  • Water 1-2 times a month
  • Best in indirect light
