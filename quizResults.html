Your Plant Soulmate

FICUS ELASTICA

Commonly known as the rubber fig, this is a low-maintenance plant that's great for sunny windows and beginners.

Water when dry, once every 1-2 weeks.
Prefers bright, direct sunlight.
Change soil once a year, preferably in spring.
Not poisonous for cats or dogs.
